# Customer Interview Guide — Data Analyst Studio

**Goal:** Validate willingness to pay and confirm Studio task relevance  
**Target:** 10–15 data analysts at small-to-mid SaaS or e-commerce  
**Duration:** 30 minutes

## Recruitment

- LinkedIn network, data Slack communities, former colleagues
- Screen: uses SQL weekly, reports to non-technical stakeholders

## Script

### Warm-up (5 min)

1. What's your current role and what does a typical week look like?
2. What tools do you use daily? (SQL, BI, Python, etc.)

### Pain discovery (10 min)

3. Where does ChatGPT or Copilot fall short for your actual work?
4. Walk me through the last time you had to investigate a metric drop. What did you do first?
5. How do you communicate findings to your VP or product team?

### Studio demo (10 min)

6. Show the Studio — one investigation task and one SQL task
7. "If you completed this 25-minute assessment and got back an agent that works like you — investigates the way you do, writes SQL your way, communicates in your style — would that be useful?"
8. "Would you pay $50/month for that? What would make it a definite yes or no?"

### Close (5 min)

9. Which Studio tasks feel most/least realistic?
10. What task would you add that would make this feel like *your* job?
11. Can I follow up when v0.5 is ready for you to try?

After 10 interviews, update `docs/product-spec.md` with:

- Confirmed persona (or narrowed sub-persona)
- Top 3 workflows to optimize Studio tasks for
- Price sensitivity ($39 vs $49 vs $79)
- Go / no-go on current Studio design
